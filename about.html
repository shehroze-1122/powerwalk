About POWERWALK

Power Walk is an energy-tech startup working on renewable sources of energy. One of our main products is energy generating tiles. Ever wondered if you can generate electricity by just walking on the road? Power Walk provides you the unique solution of doing so. The amalgam of engineering & innovation is the core of our products. Centered at NSTP-Islamabad, the startup will launch its first product soon.

OUR HISTORY

Back in 2019, we felt that there was a need of fostering the importance of sustainable products among the masses. Out of many possible options for doing so, we relied on a simple yet very striking method. These tiles not only teach people about the importance of sustainability but also provide them a chance to interact, play and produce sustainable electricity likewise. Not only that, its purpose was to encourage students to take steps towards new and nurturing technologies of the 21st century.
