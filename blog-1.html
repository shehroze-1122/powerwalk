Power Walk – An Overview

Imagine a shopping mall where all electrical appliances are powered by the footsteps of its visitors. It sounds like the future. And the future is now!

Welcome to Power Walk – a tech startup that aims to literally electrify the walking experience! Power Walk is an award-winning startup that is developing tiles that can produce electricity when trodden upon. This is the first time in Pakistan when a piezoelectric (a material that produces electric charge when mechanical stresses are applied to it) tile system is being used to generate electricity via footsteps. The tiles could be planted on heavy traffic areas such as parks, airports, malls, train and bus stations to generate maximum power, and since it is being powered by people’s walk, hence the name Power Walk.

The applications of such systems are far and wide; they could be used to power anything ranging from a household lightbulb to floodlights of a cricket stadium. The possibilities are limitless, just like the human imagination.
